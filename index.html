The movies I watched last year

Parasite

Film synopsis:
Greed and class discrimination threaten the newly formed symbiotic relationship between the wealthy Park family and the destitute Kim clan.

Director: Bong Joon Ho

Writers: Bong Joon Ho, Han Jin-won

Stars: Song Kang-ho, Lee Sun-kyun, Cho Yeo-jeong

My review:
I really like this movie, I find the little details in it really fun to find. It’s definitely one of those movies where you'll have more fun watching it for a second time. 5/5

Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania

Film synopsis:
Ant-Man and the Wasp find themselves exploring the Quantum Realm, interacting with strange new creatures and embarking on an adventure that pushes them beyond the limits of what they thought was possible.

Director: Peyton Reed

Writers: Jeff Loveness, Stan Lee, Larry Lieber

Stars: Paul Rudd, Evangeline Lilly, Michael Douglas

Movie review:
I genuinely did not enjoy watching this movie. When I first watched it, it was in the cinema. And the feeling that I treated my family to watch this movie added on to that. The movie was really bland, it feels like they asked an AI to write it. I hate it, it was so corny, not even in the funny way. 1/5

Knives Out

Film synopsis:
A detective investigates the death of the patriarch of an eccentric, combative family.

Director: Rian Johnson

Writer: Rian Johnson

Stars: Daniel Craig, Chris Evans, Ana de Armas

My review:
To be honest, the only reason why I clicked on this movie cause Daniel Craig was in it. This was the first crime-drama movie I’ve watched in a while, but I enjoyed watching it a lot. The accent Daniel Craig put up for this movie did NOT fit him at all though. 4/5
